ANATOMY

FEATURES

  1. 01

    AIR DRAFT PANEL

    Painted aluminum sheet-metal grates with an activated carbon panel behind them. The carbon panel lifts out from the top for easy replacement.

  2. 02

    PLANT HOLDERS

    PVC pipes set at a 45-degree angle. Plant roots hang inside the tower, bathed in the nutrient film.

  3. 03

    HOSE + SPINE

    The hose connects to the water pump inside the bucket. The spine secures the hose and continues the tower's form.

  4. 04

    PUMP

    A SeaFlow 10ft pump hidden inside the bucket. The power cord loops through a hole in the back.

  5. 05

    AIR INTAKE

    Circular intake modeled on standard air filter geometry, integrated seamlessly into the base.

  6. 06

    BASE PEGS

    Lock the bucket in for stability and security. 0.1mm fillets for a tight fit; the longer rear peg completes the continuous form.
